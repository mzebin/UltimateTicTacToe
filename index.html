How to Play

Normal Mode

The classic game of Tic Tac Toe. The goal is to get three of your marks (X or O) in a row, either horizontally, vertically, or diagonally.

  • Local Game: Two players take turns on the same computer.
  • Vs Computer: Play against an AI with three difficulty levels.

Ultimate Mode

A more complex version of Tic Tac Toe, played on a 3x3 grid of smaller 3x3 boards.

  • When you make a move in a small cell, you send your opponent to the corresponding sub-board. For example, if you play in the top-right cell of a sub-board, your opponent must play in the top-right sub-board.
  • If the next sub-board is already won or drawn, the opponent can play in any open cell on the board.
  • To win the game, you must win three sub-boards in a row (horizontally, vertically, or diagonally) on the main 3x3 grid.
